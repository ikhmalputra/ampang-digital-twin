# PROPOSAL

## Digital Twin Ampang Jaya & Local Authority Digital Brain

### Intelligent Command Centre for Majlis Perbandaran Ampang Jaya (MPAJ)

***

## 01. THE PROPOSAL

### Objective

Implement a Digital Twin for Ampang Jaya, providing a virtual replica of the area's physical and digital assets to enable real-time monitoring, analysis, and simulation for better decision-making under the Smart City framework.

### Key Focus Areas

- **Ampang Jaya Specific**: Concentrate on high-density residential zones, commercial corridors *(Jalan Besar Ampang, Pandan Indah, Taman Muda)*, and critical infrastructure along the Ampang LRT line
- **Public Utilities Infrastructure Sharing**: Establish dark fiber network for new development projects via Joint Venture partnership with MPAJ
- **Scope**: Buildings, roads, public spaces, and utilities within Ampang Jaya municipal boundaries

***

## 02. DIGITAL TWIN AMPANG JAYA

### Current State

- Existing MPAJ GIS/CPS planning systems provide base layer
- Current implementation limited to **2D mapping system**
- Upgrade required to **3D visualization for operational intelligence**

### Proposed 3D Conversion

#### Detailed 3D Model of Ampang Jaya

- **Residential Areas**: Taman Dagang, Taman Cahaya, Taman Sri Watan, Pandan Indah, Taman Muda, Taman Megah
- **Commercial Hubs**: Ampang Point, Pandan Kapital, Galaxy Ampang
- **Transport**: Ampang LRT stations *(Ampang, Cahaya, Cempaka, Pandan Indah, Taman Muda)*
- **Public Spaces**: Ampang Recreational Forest, Taman Tasik Tambahan, sports complexes
- **Critical Infrastructure**: Water treatment facilities, TNB substations, telecommunication towers

***

### Real-Time Data Integration

Connect sensors and IoT devices across Ampang Jaya:

- Traffic monitoring at major junctions *(Jalan Ampang/Jalan Besar intersection, MRR2 interchanges)*
- Flood sensors along **Sungai Ampang** and retention ponds
- Air quality monitors near industrial zones and highways
- Waste management tracking for residential collection routes

***

### Simulation & Predictive Analysis

- Flood modeling for Sungai Ampang catchment area
- Traffic flow simulation for LRT feeder routes
- Development impact assessment for new high-rise projects

***

## 05. SAMPLE APPLICATIONS FOR AMPANG JAYA

### A. City Overview - Geological & Environmental

- **Flood Risk Visualization**: 3D terrain model showing Sungai Ampang floodplains, retention pond capacities, and critical drainage bottlenecks
- **Air Quality Monitoring**: Real-time PM2.5/PM10 readings near MRR2, industrial areas, and construction sites

***

### B. Smart Planning

- Development control: Assess building height compliance against zoning regulations
- Land use analysis: Monitor conversion of residential to commercial *(Taman Dagang, Pandan Indah)*
- Infrastructure planning: Plan new roads and utilities *(Taman Nirwana)*

***

### C. Traffic & Transport Management

- LRT integration: Monitor feeder bus routes, parking at Ampang/Cahaya stations
- Congestion hotspots: Jalan Besar Ampang, MRR2 Pandan Interchange, Jalan Pandan Indah
- Incident response: Automated alerts, coordination with JPJ/Polis

***

### D. Security & Emergency Response

- Flood early warning: IoT sensors at Sungai Ampang
- Fire response: Nearest Bomba stations *(Ampang, Pandan Indah)*
- Evacuation planning: 3D simulation for high-density apartments

***

### E. Community Services

- Facility mapping: Service counters, halls, markets, clinics
- Public engagement: Citizen issue reporting portal
- Waste collection: Route optimization for residential areas

***

### F. Utilities Management

- Smart street lighting: LED monitoring and control
- Drainage monitoring: Blockage and overflow detection
- 5G infrastructure: Smart pole and telecom planning

***
